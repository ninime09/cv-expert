Senior Data Analytics Manager
ByteDance — Beijing / Shanghai

About the Role:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Data Analytics Manager to lead our Growth Analytics team. You will own the analytics strategy for user acquisition and retention, manage a team of 5-8 analysts, and partner closely with product, marketing, and engineering leadership.

Responsibilities:
- Define and own the analytics roadmap for Growth, including user funnel analysis, cohort analysis, and LTV modeling
- Lead and mentor a team of 5-8 data analysts and data scientists
- Partner with product managers to design and analyze A/B experiments at scale
- Build executive-facing dashboards and deliver weekly business review presentations
- Establish data governance standards and ensure data quality across the team
- Drive adoption of self-serve analytics tools across the organization
- Collaborate with data engineering to design scalable data pipelines

Requirements:
- 5+ years of experience in data analytics, business intelligence, or data science
- 2+ years of people management experience
- Expert-level SQL and proficiency in Python or R
- Deep experience with A/B testing and causal inference methods
- Strong experience with BI tools (Tableau, Looker, or equivalent)
- Proven track record of delivering business impact through data
- Excellent communication skills — able to present complex findings to C-suite

Preferred:
- Experience at a high-growth internet company (e-commerce, social media, fintech)
- Familiarity with growth frameworks (AARRR, North Star Metric)
- Experience with large-scale distributed systems (Spark, Hive, Presto)
- MBA or advanced degree preferred

About ByteDance:
ByteDance is the parent company of TikTok, Douyin, and other globally recognized products. We are a mission-driven company committed to inspiring creativity and bringing joy.
