Data Analyst Intern

- Familiarity with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Understanding of A/B testing and statistical hypothesis testing
- Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail

Nice to Have:

- Knowledge of machine learning fundamentals
- Prior internship experience in analytics or data science
- Familiarity with AWS (S3, Athena, Redshift)
